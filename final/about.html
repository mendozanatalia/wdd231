What is "Petita Regalos"?

Petita Regalos offers handmade gifts, mainly custom amigurumis (crocheted stuffed animals), as well as artisan jewelry and handcrafted stationery. All products are made to order with attention to detail and personal touches.

A Litte bit of Our Story

The name Petita Regalos is a heartfelt tribute to the founder’s mother, lovingly nicknamed "Petita"—a term of endearment inspired by the way turtles are affectionately called in her home country. The name reflects warmth, care, and the deep personal meaning behind each creation. “Regalos” clearly conveys what we offer: thoughtful, handmade gifts crafted with love and intention.

What is Our Purpose?

We provide these handmade products to people looking for meaningful, personalized gifts—whether for birthdays, special occasions, or just to show appreciation. Our customers value craftsmanship, emotional connection, and one-of-a-kind creations.
