Name

The name Petita Regalos is a heartfelt tribute to our mother, nicknamed "Petita" when she was a child —a term inspired by the way turtles are affectionately called in our home country. The name reflects warmth, care, and the deep personal meaning for us behind each creation. “Regalos” ties it all together by expressing what we offer: unique, handcrafted gifts made with love.

Domain:

www.petitaregalos.com.bo

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to introduce "Petita Regalos", a handmade gift small bussiness, offering primarily amigurumis, as well as handcrafted jewelry and stationery. Through this site, visitors can learn who we are, our story, and explore our product catalog. We aim to connect with those who value handmade creations, inspiring trust and encouraging thoughtful purchases by sharing the story behind our items.
